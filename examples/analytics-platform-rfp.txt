REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Program: State Analytics and Compliance Reporting Platform
Issued By: Department of Public Administration

Purpose:
The department seeks a secure analytics platform to unify operational reporting, compliance audit evidence,
and executive dashboards across multiple agencies.

Functional Requirements:
- Consolidated reporting across finance, procurement, and citizen service data sets
- Role-based access controls with strict audit logging
- Scheduled and ad-hoc dashboard publishing
- Data retention and legal hold policy support

Technical Expectations:
- Cloud-native architecture with encrypted data at rest and in transit
- ETL/ELT pipeline orchestration
- Integration with enterprise identity provider
- High-availability deployment and disaster recovery plan

Security and Compliance:
- SOC2-aligned controls
- Regulatory audit traceability
- Automated alerting for suspicious access patterns

Timeline:
- Vendor selection in 6 weeks
- MVP delivery in 4 months
- Full rollout in 8 months

Risks Called Out by Department:
- Incomplete source system data quality documentation
- Potential delays in legal and compliance sign-off cycles
- Scope creep risk from additional agency onboarding requests

Deliverables:
- Solution architecture and implementation roadmap
- Working analytics platform
- Compliance evidence package
- Operations runbook and training materials
