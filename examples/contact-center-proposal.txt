PROPOSAL
Project: Healthcare Contact Center Transformation
Prepared For: Meridian Health Network
Prepared By: BrightPath Digital

Executive Summary:
BrightPath proposes a phased modernization of Meridian's patient contact center operations to improve service speed,
quality, and reporting visibility.

Proposed Outcomes:
- Shorter patient wait times
- Improved first-contact resolution
- Enhanced care navigation quality

Approach:
Phase 1: discovery and operating model alignment
Phase 2: platform implementation and integrations
Phase 3: adoption, training, and optimization

Solution Components:
- Omnichannel contact platform
- CRM integration for patient context
- Workflow automation for callbacks and escalations
- Analytics dashboards for service leaders

Key Stakeholders:
- VP Patient Services
- Contact Center Director
- Clinical Operations Representative
- Data and Integration Lead

Technology Stack:
Cloud contact center platform, API integration layer, reporting warehouse, BI dashboards.

Dependencies and Constraints:
- Access to current call routing configuration
- Availability of integration SMEs during design workshops
- Final data retention policy approval from governance board

Risk Considerations:
- Adoption risk if training is delayed
- Data mapping issues between legacy systems and new CRM model
- Possible timeline pressure if integration decisions are deferred
