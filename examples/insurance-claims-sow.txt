STATEMENT OF WORK (SOW)
Project: Insurance Claims Workflow Rebuild
Client: NorthStar Mutual Insurance

Overview:
NorthStar requires redesign of its claims intake and adjudication workflow. The existing process is email-driven,
contains duplicate data entry, and causes delays in high-volume periods.

Objectives:
- Reduce claim intake cycle time by 25 percent
- Improve data quality and auditability
- Standardize approval and escalation workflows

Workstreams:
1) Process discovery and user journey mapping
2) New claims portal and case management screens
3) Backend workflow engine and rules automation
4) Integration with policy administration APIs
5) Reporting dashboards for operations leadership

Technology Expectations:
React frontend, FastAPI services, PostgreSQL, Redis queueing, containerized deployment on Kubernetes.

Timeline:
- Discovery: 3 weeks
- Build and integration: 9 weeks
- UAT and hardening: 4 weeks
- Production rollout: 2 weeks
Total estimated duration: 18 weeks.

Stakeholders:
- Program Director, Claims Operations
- Enterprise Architect
- Security Governance Lead
- Data Engineering Manager

Dependencies:
- Access to legacy policy APIs
- Data mapping sign-off from data governance council
- UAT environment readiness before week 10

Acceptance Criteria:
Must include measurable metrics for first-pass adjudication success and exception queue reduction.

Risks:
Potential delays if legacy API rate limits are not adjusted before full volume testing.
