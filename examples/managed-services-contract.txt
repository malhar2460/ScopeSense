MASTER SERVICES CONTRACT
Agreement: Managed IT Support and Operations Renewal
Client: Apex Retail Group
Vendor: Orbit Systems LLP

Contract Term:
Initial 24-month term with optional 12-month extension.

Scope of Services:
- 24x7 incident monitoring and response
- Infrastructure maintenance and patching
- On-call production support and outage remediation
- Monthly service performance reporting

Service Levels (SLA):
- Critical incident response in 15 minutes
- Priority-1 resolution target of 4 hours
- Monthly uptime target: 99.95%

Commercial and Legal Terms:
- Service credits apply for SLA breaches
- Penalty schedule escalates after three consecutive months of missed uptime targets
- Vendor indemnifies client for data breach attributable to vendor negligence
- Contract may be terminated for repeated material non-compliance

Stakeholders and Ownership:
- Client IT Operations Director
- Vendor Delivery Manager
- Security and Compliance Officer
- Legal Counsel Review Board

Dependencies:
- Shared observability tooling access
- Incident escalation matrix approved by both parties
- Quarterly security audit completion

Risk Highlights:
- High legal exposure on breach-related indemnity clauses
- Ambiguity in responsibility split for third-party cloud incidents
- Potential dispute on SLA measurement windows if monitoring tools are inconsistent
